Courier

DachsCourier – offers customers a peace of mind

DachsCourier works with FedEx, TNT, DHL and StarTrack for domestic and international express shipping services. Each carrier’s services are different, and we specialise in finding the best option to meet your delivery requirement. We also ensure that your items are prepared in a way that is easy for the courier company to handle, this reduces the risk of potential issues that might arise during the transit.


  • Service oriented
  • Handle with care
  • Multiple options for different levels of services
  • Punctual delivery
  • Tracking available for sender and receiver

Domestic Express

Same day Express

Our same-day express service is available in Melbourne, Sydney, Brisbane, Adelaide and Perth metro areas, turnaround time is between two- and four-hours during business days.

Overnight Express

Overnight express services are available between capital cities. In some cases, we can deliver by next day 9 am or 12 noon, depending on the location.

Road Express

Road express is most economical, perfect for bulk items or delivery to a regional area. We can send from anywhere in Australia to anywhere in Australia with Road Express service.

Domestic Express

We can send a wide range of items and ensure that your shipment is in compliance with regulations.

Regular items

Documents, clothing and other personal belongings, commercial samples.

Biological samples

Blood sample, saliva sample, urine sample and hair sample.

Dangerous Items

devices with a lithium battery – mobile phone, laptops and other electronic devices.



Packing service

Packing is important before sending your items to travel. If you do not have the proper equipment and material to do the packing, we can help! We have the experience to pack electronic equipment, paintings and fragile items such as glassware or woodware.
