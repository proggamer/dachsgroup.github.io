Dachs Design

Graphic design for print media is quite different from that for online media. It is very important that the designer is experienced in print media. Otherwise incorrect settings, for example in colour setting or in finish setting, can potentially result in a disaster for your print project. DachsDesign is efficient and experienced with professional graphic design service for print media.

In order to achieve the best quality of the print, a great graphic design is absolutely essential to begin with. Our in-house design service is inexpensive and has many years of real design experience across different industries such as:

  • Healthcare/Aged care
  • Education
  • Information Technology
  • Agriculture
  • Financial
  • Legal
