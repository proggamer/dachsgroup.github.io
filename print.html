Books & Booklets

Perfect Bound booklets

Perfect bound books are great for publications with 30 pages or more. The cover is folded twice to form a perfectly square spine which the pages are glued into. This gives the book a clean, professional look. Available sizes include A4, A5 and custom size upon request. Perfect binding is perfect for annual report and other formally presentable collaterals.​

Saddle stitched booklets

Saddle-stitched booklet is perfect for lower page count projects, like catalogues, manuals, pamphlets, or event programs. The booklet pages are required to be in the multiple of four (4), and are stapled through the fold line. Available sizes include A4, A5 and custom size upon request.

Wiro Bound booklets

Wiro bound booklets are bound with a sturdy metal coil, allowing pages to lay flat. Our wiro coil’s size is maxed at around 40mm. It’s great for training manuals and planners.

Comb Bound booklets

Comb binding uses plastic combs to bind the book, similar to wiro bound, the books can stay flat. Largest size of combs is at around 60mm. It’s great for contracts, manuals and legal documents.

NCR books

NCR form printing plays an important role in business. Almost every transaction involves the use of forms. Companies in manufacturing or insurance record invoices, receipts, and inventory logs. NCR forms lessen the hassle of filling out several copies of a document every time.

Each set provides several copies of one form. Pressure applied with a pen on the first sheet copies the markings onto the paper underneath. This removes the need to use carbon copy paper, enabling more time-efficient transactions.
